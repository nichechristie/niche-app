# "Find Your Faith & NICHE" Hoodie Design Specification

## Design Concept
A premium hoodie that bridges faith and the creator economy with powerful messaging on both front and back.

## Front Design

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║                                      ║
║        Find your faith               ║
║                                      ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝
```

### Front Typography
- **Text**: "Find your faith"
- **Font**: Bold Sans-Serif (Montserrat Bold, Bebas Neue)
- **Size**: Medium (approx 36-48pt for print)
- **Position**: Centered on chest, 3-4 inches down from collar
- **Width**: 8-10 inches
- **Style**: Clean, minimalist approach

## Back Design

```
╔══════════════════════════════════════╗
║                                      ║
║         Find your NICHE              ║
║                                      ║
╚══════════════════════════════════════╝
```

### Back Typography
- **Text**: "Find your NICHE"
- **Font**: Same as front (Montserrat Bold, Bebas Neue)
- **Size**: Larger than front (approx 48-60pt for print)
- **Position**: Upper back, centered between shoulder blades
- **Width**: 10-12 inches
- **Style**: Bold statement piece, "NICHE" can be emphasized

## Color Palettes by Hoodie Color

### Option 1: Black Hoodie
- **Front Text**: White (#FFFFFF) or Gold (#FFD700)
- **Back Text**: Same as front, or use contrasting option (White front, Gold back)
- **Effect**: Subtle outer glow for depth

### Option 2: Charcoal Hoodie
- **Front Text**: White (#FFFFFF) or Light Gray (#E5E7EB)
- **Back Text**: White with subtle shadow
- **Effect**: Clean, modern look

### Option 3: Purple Hoodie
- **Front Text**: White (#FFFFFF) or Gold (#FFD700)
- **Back Text**: White with gold outline
- **Effect**: Premium, bold contrast

### Option 4: Navy Hoodie
- **Front Text**: White (#FFFFFF) or Light Blue (#87CEEB)
- **Back Text**: White or Gold
- **Effect**: Classic, clean aesthetic

## Design Elements

### Front Design Options
1. **Simple Text Only** (Recommended)
   - Clean typography, no additional graphics
   - Let the message stand on its own

2. **Text with Small Cross**
   - Small cross (1-2 inches) centered below or above text
   - Subtle, not overpowering

3. **Text with Subtle Icon**
   - Small praying hands or faith symbol
   - Positioned near text

### Back Design Options
1. **Bold Typography Only** (Recommended)
   - Large, statement-making text
   - No additional graphics needed

2. **Text with Logo**
   - Small "Find Your Niche" logo below main text
   - Website or social handle

3. **Text with Accent Line**
   - Horizontal line above or below text
   - Adds visual interest

## Placement & Sizing

### Front Placement
- **Vertical**: 3-4 inches down from bottom of hood/collar
- **Horizontal**: Centered on chest
- **Print Area**: 8-10 inches wide × 3-4 inches tall

### Back Placement
- **Vertical**: 4-5 inches down from collar seam
- **Horizontal**: Centered on back
- **Print Area**: 10-12 inches wide × 4-6 inches tall

## Print Specifications

### Printing Method
- **Screen Printing**: Best for solid colors, durable
- **DTG (Direct to Garment)**: Good for detailed designs
- **Embroidery**: Premium option for text (consider for "faith")

### Hoodie Specifications
- **Material**: 80% cotton, 20% polyester fleece
- **Weight**: Medium-heavy (8-9 oz)
- **Style**: Pullover hoodie with kangaroo pocket
- **Hood**: Drawstring hood
- **Cuffs**: Ribbed cuffs and waistband

## Production Notes
- Ensure text is properly centered on both front and back
- Account for hood/seam placement to avoid text distortion
- Test print on hoodie material for color accuracy
- Consider offering both pullover and zip-up versions
- Quality control: Check alignment and text clarity

## Sizing Guidance
- S: 20" chest width, 27" length
- M: 22" chest width, 28" length
- L: 24" chest width, 29" length
- XL: 26" chest width, 30" length
- 2XL: 28" chest width, 31" length

---

**Design Philosophy**: This hoodie represents the dual journey of finding your spiritual path (faith) and discovering your purpose in the creator economy (NICHE). The front-to-back design creates a complete message that resonates with creators building on Base while staying grounded in their beliefs.
