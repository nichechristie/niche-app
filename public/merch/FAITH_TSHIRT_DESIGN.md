### Cross Design Element
**Primary Feature**: Large, bold cross centered below the text

**Cross Style Options**:
1. **Modern Minimalist Cross**: Clean lines, simple geometry
   - Width: 4-5 inches
   - Thickness: 0.5-0.75 inches
   - Style: Solid fill or outlined

2. **Rugged Cross**: Wooden texture, slightly weathered look
   - More detailed, shows wood grain
   - Symbolic of the Calvary cross
   - Works great on dark shirts

3. **Geometric Cross**: Made of intersecting shapes/blocks
   - Modern, tech-forward aesthetic
   - Bridges faith and blockchain themes
   - Clean, architectural lines

## Typography Recommendations

### Primary Text: "Find your NICHE,"
- Font: Bold Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat Bold, Bebas Neue)
- Size: Large (approx 48-60pt for print)
- Color Options by shirt color:
  - Black shirt: White or Gold text
  - White shirt: Black or Navy text
  - Heather Gray: Black or Deep Purple
  - Navy: White or Gold text

### Emphasized Text: "FIND your faith"
- Font: Same as primary, ALL CAPS
- Size: Slightly larger or same as "NICHE" line
- Weight: Extra Bold
- Effect: Subtle glow or outline for emphasis

### Cross Element (Primary Design Feature)
- **Position**: Centered below text, main focal point after typography
- **Size**: 4-5 inches tall, proportional width
- **Spacing**: 1-2 inches below "faith" text
- **Style**: Match shirt color palette (see color options below)
- **Optional**: Subtle shadow or depth effect for dimension
